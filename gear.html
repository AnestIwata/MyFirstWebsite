Skydiving gear!

Parachute

As a main parachute I jump Icon container manufatured by Aerodyne Research LLC. The Icon ‘V’ is loaded with all the available options and was designed with high speed flying in mind.

Canopy

My main canopy is Icarus Crossfire 2 149 sqf. The Crossfire represents a massive leap forward in performance from any other 9 cell in the world.

Helmet

Cookie fuel is my choice of headgear. Cookie's Fuel open faced skydiving helmet is designed to allow maximum customization the Fuel helmet renders the possibility to choose a number of accessories for cameras, altimeters and cutaway systems.
